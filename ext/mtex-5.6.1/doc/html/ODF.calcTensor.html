calcTensor

compute the average tensor for an ODF

Syntax 

[TVoigt, TReuss, THill] = calcTensor(odf,T)
THill = calcTensor(odf,T,'Hill')
TGeo = calcTensor(odf,T,'geometric')

Input

odf ODF
T tensor

Output

T tensor

Options

Voigt Boigt mean
Reuss Reuss mean
Hill Hill mean
geometric geometric mean

See also

tensor.mean, EBSD.calcTensor
